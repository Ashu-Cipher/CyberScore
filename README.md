# CyberScore

## 📖 Description
* This is a site to assess your Cyber Security level.
* It has **4 Levels**.
* **40 real-world scenarios** to assess your score.
* **Multiple Common Attack** like Phishing, Social Engineering, Brute Force included.
* This is made for #horizons.

## ⚙️ Tech Stack

* **Vanilla JS:** Engine, Logic, Screen management
* **Inline SVGs:** Animation crisp
* **AI Usage**: Used AI for some question idea, and for some debugging.

## 🎯 Motivation
* **Increasing Cyber Attack**
* **Lack of Awareness**
* **My Love for Cyber Security**

## 🛠️ How It Works
* **The Logic**: Starts from 500 point. you gain/lose after every question
* **Scenario Database**: Holds 40 scenarios and it's explaination.
* **Category Tracking**: Keep track and tell your weak area with result.

## 🛠️ How To Use?
1.**Open Link**: Open Link associated with this repo.  
2. **Start Assessment**: Click Start Assessment.  
3. **Complete Quiz**: Complete quiz as per your knowledge.  
4. **Result**: Check Your score, find your weak spot.  
5. **Implement**:Fix your weak spot.
